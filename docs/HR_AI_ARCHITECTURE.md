↓

↓

---

## Modules

### Resume Parser
Extracts resume text.

### ATS Engine
Calculates ATS score.

### AI Screening
Generates screening questions.

### HR Interview
Conducts AI interview.

### Communication Analyzer
Analyzes communication quality.

### Confidence Analyzer
Measures confidence.

### HR Scoring Engine
Generates HR interview score.

### Unified Scoring
Combines ATS + Screening + HR scores.

### Final Recommendation
Hire / Further Interview / Reject
